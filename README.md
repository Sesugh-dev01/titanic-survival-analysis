Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Titanic passenger dataset to identify
the key factors that determined survival.

## What I did
- Loaded and inspected 891 passenger records
- Identified and handled missing values in Age (20%) and Cabin (77%)
- Visualised survival rates by gender, passenger class, and age group
- Summarised findings in plain English

## Key findings
- Women had a significantly higher survival rate than men across all classes
- First-class passengers survived at more than twice the rate of third-class
- The Cabin column was dropped due to 77% missing data

## Tools used
Python · Pandas · Matplotlib · Seaborn · Jupyter Notebook

## Dataset
[Titanic dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/titanic) — Kaggle
